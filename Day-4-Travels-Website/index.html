About Bihar – The Land of Heritage, Spirituality & Culture

Bihar, the heart of India’s ancient civilization, is a land where history, spirituality, and natural beauty come together to create a mesmerizing travel experience. Known as the birthplace of Buddhism and Jainism, Bihar has been a center of knowledge, culture, and governance for centuries. From the ruins of Nalanda and Vikramshila universities to the sacred sites of Bodh Gaya and Vaishali, Bihar offers a unique blend of heritage and enlightenment
